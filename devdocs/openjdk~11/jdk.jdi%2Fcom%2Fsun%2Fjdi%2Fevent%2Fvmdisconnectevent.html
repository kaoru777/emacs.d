public interface VMDisconnectEvent
extends Event

Notification of disconnection from target VM. May be caused by normal termination of a VM, VM termination by uncaught exception or other error, debugger action (VirtualMachine.dispose() or VirtualMachine.exit(int)) or by external events (for example, target process termination by the operating system, transport termination, etc).

If the target VM terminates before the disconnection, this event will be preceded by a VMDeathEvent.

This event is always sent. There is no corresponding EventRequest. The enclosing singleton EventSet always has a suspend policy of EventRequest.SUSPEND_NONE.
